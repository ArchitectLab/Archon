The bowtie is inherited from Morphaius: a spectrum stroke crossing through itself — the idea passing through every colour before it becomes real. It sits on the architect's grid and never tilts.

Stacked
Do

Keep the spectrum running bottom-left → top-right, green to magenta. Give the mark clear space equal to half its height. Minimum size 24 px — favicon uses the mark alone.

Don't

Don't tilt, stretch or reverse the spectrum. In single-colour contexts use the mono lockup (white on dark, Deep Void on light) — never one isolated spectrum colour on the mark.
