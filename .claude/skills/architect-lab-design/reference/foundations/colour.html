// 03 — la palette

The spectrum & the void

Four signal colours that only ever live in a gradient — on strokes, bars, rules and the logo, never as a massive flat fill. The grounds stay neutral and dark; the contrast between the quiet grid and the vibrating spectrum is the identity.
